About our hotel

Since opening our doors in 1991, Best Hotel has been welcoming travelers from all corners of the world, serving as a reliable sanctuary where timeless Kazakh hospitality meets modern luxury. Witnessing the beautiful transformation of Almaty over the decades, we have continuously refined our services to remain a preferred choice for both discerning business executives and leisure seekers.

We are perfectly situated in the vibrant heart of the city, offering our guests effortless access to historic landmarks, cultural centers, and premier dining, all while being framed by the breathtaking, snow-capped peaks of the Tien Shan mountains. Every square meter of our property is designed with your ultimate peace in mind, featuring elegantly appointed rooms, high-speed connectivity, and tranquil wellness spaces. We believe that true hospitality lies in the details, which is why our dedicated team works around the clock to provide deeply personalized care.

Nestled in one of Almaty's most picturesque locations, Best Hotel offers breathtaking views of the majestic Tian Shan mountains and the vibrant city skyline. Every morning begins with fresh mountain air and unforgettable scenery, creating the perfect atmosphere for relaxation, inspiration, and unforgettable memories.

Our convenient location allows guests to reach Almaty's most popular attractions, shopping centers, restaurants, business districts, and cultural landmarks within minutes. Whether you're planning a business trip, a family vacation, or a romantic getaway, everything you need is always close by.

Explore scenic parks, hiking trails, ski resorts, and local cafés before returning to the comfort of spacious rooms designed for rest and tranquility. After a day of adventure or meetings, unwind while enjoying panoramic views and the warm hospitality that has welcomed travelers since 1991.
